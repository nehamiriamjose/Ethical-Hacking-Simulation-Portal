🔓 Offensive Security Testing

Account Takeover & Money Transfer Lab

What is Offensive Security Testing?

Offensive security (ethical hacking) involves simulating attacks on systems to find vulnerabilities BEFORE malicious actors do. It's like testing your home's security by finding the weakest locks—ethically.

🎯

Why It Matters

Find bugs before attackers. Prevent financial loss. Meet compliance requirements. Protect user data. Stay ahead of threats.

💼

Real-World Risk

Account takeover and unauthorized money transfers cost organizations millions annually. Banks lose $16B+ to fraud yearly.

🛡️

Learn to Defend

By understanding attack methods, you become better at preventing them. Knowledge is your strongest defense.

In This Lab

Objective: Exploit multiple vulnerabilities to gain unauthorized access and transfer money illegally.

Vulnerabilities:

  • SQL Injection: Bypass authentication via SQL comments
  • Broken Authorization: Transfer money from any account
  • Weak Session Management: Sessions easily hijacked
  • No CSRF Protection: Automatic request forgery
  • Information Disclosure: All accounts visible publicly
